About Me

Mike Lewis

At the age of 6, I wrote my very first computer program on our family's new home computer. I was instantly hooked, and computers became my second hobby, next to my toy and model trains.

Although I originally planned to get a degree in computer science my first time through college, I was sidetracked into getting an associate's degree in graphic design. It has treated me well, but now I want to finish what I started. The timing was right as my previous employer went bankrupt right after I finished completing the Pathway Connect program.

I recently accepted a position as a technology facilitator for a business IT services company. It fits nicely with my pursuit for my bachelor's degree, which I expect to complete later next year.
