About six years after my very first programming experience, I finally wrote a useful application. This was done at the request of my father who was working many long hours in private practice as an optometrist. Unfortunately, some of his patients were way behind on their bills, and he needed a calculator to figure out what was owed after factoring in interest, processing, and other fees, as well as how long the bill was past due.
