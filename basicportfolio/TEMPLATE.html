This is the very first program I wrote entirely on my own based on what I had learned. It is definitely the nonsense of a 6-year old. I knew how to output messages to the screen, display the built-in graphics characters, and change colors of the text, background, and screen border.

I also knew how to get input from the user, but I wasn't quite sure what to do with it.
